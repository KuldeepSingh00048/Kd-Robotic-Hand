Robotic Hand

About this Project

This project is about robotics, we make just a supplement of robot's body part that its hand. It will work by copying our hand. We will move our finger in front of webcam then it take our finger movement as input and work in respective manner. The robotic hand is made up of a prosthetic hand with the help of 3d printer.

The methodology of this project is to enable robots to perform such a intellectual task such as decision making, problem solving perception understanding and human communication,
To replace human effort from:
1.Repetitive tasks
2.Human critical life in surgery , space missions, etc.
3.Making health care domain more efficient
